I'm a software engineer with 5 years of experience building things and occasionally breaking them (intentionally, of course—security research counts). When I'm not writing code, you'll find me playing guitar or learning something new. I believe in the power of mentorship, both giving and receiving it.

In this blog I'll be sharing things I'm learning, learned, learnt, projects I'm working on, security research, development insights, and probably some thoughts on music and life in between. Think of it as a digital notebook that happens to be public.

Why share? Because the best way to solidify your own understanding is to explain it to others. Plus, if something I write helps even one person solve a problem or learn something new, that's worth it.

Expect posts about:
- Software development and engineering
- Security research (with my mentor)
- Tools and workflows
- Lessons learned (both the easy, and hard ways)
- MAYBE some guitar-related tangents

No fixed schedule, just content when I have something worth sharing.

Thanks for stopping by. Let's learn together.
